about me

I'm a senior at Indiana University where I'm currently working as the creative director of the Indiana Daily Student. There, I oversee a staff of designers and photographers and lead them in producing engaging work both for online and print. (On special occasions, like astronomical phenomena, I'll grab a notepad and report.) When I'm not in the newsroom, I can either be found listening to this album on loop, calculating an astrological birth chart or wishing I knew how to skateboard.
