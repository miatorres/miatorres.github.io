about

I been designing pages at the Washington Post since June — first as an intern and now as a contractor. I've worked in just about every section — from Metro to Local Living to Sports. Between print shifts, I've gotten to work on digital projects and get into art direction. Before the Post, I worked at the Indiana Daily Student, Indiana University's independent, student-run newspaper, as creative director. I'm a New York Times Student Journalism Institute alumna and a proud National Association of Hispanic Journalists member. When I'm not in the newsroom, I can be found listening to this album on loop, calculating an astrological birth chart or wishing I knew how to skateboard.
